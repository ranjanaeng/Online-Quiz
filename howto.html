How to Take the Quiz

  1. Click Start Quiz.
  2. Read the question and select your answer:
    • Multiple-choice / True–False: choose one option.
    • Fill‑in‑the‑blank: type your answer (case-insensitive).
  3. Use the Next button to go to the following question.
  4. On the last question, click Submit to see your results.
  5. The results page shows your score, a breakdown, and explanations.

Accessibility Notes

  • All controls are keyboard accessible (Tab/Shift+Tab, Arrow keys for radios).
  • High-contrast color palette for readability.
  • Alt text for non-text graphics and semantic landmarks (header, main, nav, footer).
